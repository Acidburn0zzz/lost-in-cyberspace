Lost in Cyberspace

This is a cooperative game designed for 2 players playing on separate devices.
The only connection you need between each other is your voice. You can be in the same room or on the other part of the world as long as you can hear each other.

One player takes a role of a HACKER lost in the cyberspace maze of a corporate network. Their role is to move around cyberspace, read access codes to NAVIGATOR, find a target node and hack it.

  • Play as HACKER [VR]

The other player takes a role of a NAVIGATOR who uses terminal to decode access codes they got from HACKER to read the map of cyberspace network HACKER is trapped in and help them find the way to the target node.

  • Play as NAVIGATOR [Desktop]
